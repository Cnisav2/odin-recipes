Spaghetti

Description

In the mood for comfort food? Give this elevated classic a try!

Easy enough to get the kids involved in the process!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 lbs cooked spaghetti noodles
  • 1 head garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 lemon juiced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in large skillet and add garlic - simmer for 5 minutes
  2. Sprinkle in crushed red pepper and lemon juice - heat for approximately 2 minutes
  3. Toss cookec pasta in skillet
  4. Sprinkle cheese over finished result
  5. Enjoy!
