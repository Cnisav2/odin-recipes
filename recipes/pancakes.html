Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/s cup flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/3 tablespoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix and add milk, butter, and egg
  2. Heat pan and scoop batter into pan, 1/4 cup at a time
  3. Cook until bubbles form and edges are dry, 2-3 minutes
  4. Flip and cook until browned
  5. Repeat and enjoy
